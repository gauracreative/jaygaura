Oh, hey—you found me.

Name: Jay Gaura a.k.a. Visnujan das

What I do: IT guy, working remotely

Where I live: Gorgeous rural Texas

I created this page as a space to explore and express some of the ideas that have been rattling around in my head for years. It’s an experiment, really—let’s see where it goes.

The first thing I’ve always been fascinated by is happiness. Ever since I was young, I’ve wondered what it’s made of, how it works, and whether some people seem to have more of it than others. Those questions eventually led me to the Bhakti tradition, which has been central to my life for many years. That’s one of the things I’ll be sharing about under Bhakti • Personal Development.

Another big topic I’m interested in is how we interact with the world and each other. Can we really be happy in isolation, or do we need to find a way to coexist and thrive together? This opens up a whole range of questions about society, community, education, culture and how we aim for peace and harmony. So, the second focus will be World • Peace • Harmony.

Finally, I’m an IT guy. I love tech, creating efficient systems, and making life easier through smart automation. Over the years, I've designed solutions that save time, cut costs, and keep projects running smoothly. I'll be sharing insights and experiences from my work here, under IT • Business.
